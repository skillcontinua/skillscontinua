THE ORGANISATION
Renaissance Stream Foundation is a West African NGO dedicated to
economic empowerment through education, digital skills and community
knowledge systems. Our platforms serve farming, trading, fishing,
transportation and traditional knowledge communities across Africa.

Current active platforms:
- mkomigbo.com/awag/ — Agricultural and trading calendar serving
  Igbo and West African farming and fishing communities
- mkomigbo.com/igbo-calendar/ — Traditional Igbo calendar system
  serving cultural and commercial communities
- skillscontinua.com — Vocational and life skills e-learning
  platform (in final development)

THE PROBLEM
Sierra Leone has one of the highest youth unemployment rates in West
Africa. Most working-age Sierra Leoneans have no access to affordable,
practical skills training. The formal education system does not equip
people for the real economy. Petty traders — the backbone of the
Sierra Leonean economy — lose money daily because they do not know
how to calculate the true cost of their goods or keep basic records.
Skilled tradespeople have no recognised certification pathway.

THE SOLUTION — SKILLSCONTINUA
SkillsContinua is a free online learning platform delivering
practical, certificate-bearing courses to anyone with a smartphone
or computer and internet access.

Current platform status (development complete):
- 77 courses across 8 pillars
- 386 lessons with full text content
- Certificate system with Gold (Distinction), Silver (Merit)
  and Bronze (Pass) ratings
- Quiz and assessment system (170 questions completed,
  expanding to full coverage)
- Courses in: Vocational trades, Computer literacy, Secondary
  education, Primary education, Life skills, Financial literacy,
  Entrepreneurship and Professional certifications

Sierra Leone specific content:
- Trading and Petty Business Management
  (true cost of goods — the critical knowledge gap)
- Simple Bookkeeping for Small Businesses
- Value-Added Business and Market Trading
- Fishing and Fish Processing (Freetown coastal communities)
- Understanding the Leone and Inflation
- Krio for Business Communication
- Port and Logistics Work
