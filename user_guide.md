# SkillsContinua - User Guide

## 👋 Welcome to SkillsContinua

SkillsContinua is a comprehensive digital skills and vocational training platform designed to take you from where you are to where you want to be.

## 📚 Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account
1. Visit [skillscontinua.com]
2. Click "Register" in the top menu
3. Fill in your details
4. Verify your email

### 2. Choose Your Learning Path
- Browse available courses
- Filter by category or learning approach
- Select courses that interest you

### 3. Start Learning
- Click "Enroll" on any course
- Access lessons, videos, and quizzes
- Track your progress

## 🎓 Learning Approaches

| Approach | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| **Pedagogic** | Children (6-12 years) |
| **Andragogic** | Adults (18+) |
| **Heutagogic** | Advanced/Self-directed learners |
| **Cybergogic** | Tech-enhanced learning |

## 📖 Course Structure

Each course includes:
- **10+ lessons** with video content
- **Quizzes** to test your knowledge
- **Practical exercises** to apply your skills
- **Certification** upon completion

## 🌍 Language Support

SkillsContinua supports 6 languages:
- English 🇬🇧

- Spanish 🇪🇸
- Portuguese 🇵🇹

To change language, click the globe icon in the top menu.

## 🎯 Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see:
- **Overview:** Your progress summary
- **My Courses:** All enrolled courses
- **Statistics:** Courses completed, in progress
- **Certificates:** Earned certificates

## 🏆 Earning Certificates

1. Complete all lessons in a course
2. Pass all quizzes (70%+)
3. Complete practical exercises
4. Your certificate will be generated automatically

## 💡 Tips for Success

1. **Set a schedule** - Dedicate regular time to learning
2. **Take notes** - Write down key concepts
3. **Practice regularly** - Apply what you learn
4. **Join discussions** - Learn from other students
5. **Ask questions** - Use the support channels

## ❓ FAQ

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Login" → "Forgot Password" → Enter your email

### Can I access courses offline?
Yes, you can download lesson materials for offline use.

### How long do I have access to courses?
Once enrolled, you have lifetime access to course materials.

### Are certificates recognized?
Yes, certificates are verifiable and recognized by partner organizations.
